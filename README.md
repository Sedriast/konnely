<h1 align="center">
Development of cross-platform software for the management of data in the rabbit farming process at the agro-environmental unit El Tíbar.</h1>

<h2 align="center">
    
> Konnely
>
> Sebastián Blanco Castañeda and Juan Felipe Alonso Robayo

</h2>
<h3 align="left">
In the agro-environmental unit of Tíbar, through support for resources, plans, programs, and projects for agricultural and livestock technical assistance in the municipality of Ubaté, the need for technological extension projects capable of efficiently managing the data generated by the implemented rabbit farming process is evident.

In doing so, tools are created from the field of systems engineering to provide solutions to issues present in other areas of knowledge.
</h3>
